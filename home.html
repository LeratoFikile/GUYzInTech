RoboCodes

About the Project

RoboCodes is an AI-powered, gamified educational tool designed to teach children coding and robotics in a fun, accessible way. The platform brings tech education to life in schools and communities where it's needed most, making complex concepts approachable through interactive lessons and challenges.

Our Inspiration

We noticed a significant gap in tech education in underserved communities. Many children never get exposure to coding or robotics, putting them at a disadvantage in our increasingly digital world. RoboCodes was born from our desire to democratize tech education and inspire the next generation of innovators.

QhaweLami

About the Project

QhaweLami (meaning "My Hero" in isiXhosa) is a gamified AI-powered web app built to combat gender-based violence by educating and empowering users through interactive learning. The platform provides resources, real-time assistance, and educational games that teach about healthy relationships and personal safety.

Our Inspiration

Alarmed by South Africa's high rates of gender-based violence, we wanted to create a tool that could educate and empower individuals while being accessible and engaging. The gamification approach helps break down barriers to discussing difficult topics and makes learning about personal safety more approachable.

AquaGuard

About the Project

AquaGuard is an IoT-powered gamified web app that helps users monitor their water usage and learn how to manage their water consumption in real-time. The system gathers data from smart water meters, provides actionable insights, and rewards conservation efforts through a fun, competitive interface.

Our Inspiration

With increasing water scarcity in many regions, we wanted to create a solution that would make water conservation engaging and rewarding. Traditional water monitoring systems often go unused because they're not engaging - AquaGuard changes that by making water conservation a game with real-world impact.
